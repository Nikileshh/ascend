# PRODUCT SPECIFICATION

## Product Name

LifeOS AI

Tagline:

Turn your goals into a personalized execution system.

---

# Vision

LifeOS AI is an intelligent productivity platform.

Unlike habit trackers that simply record actions, LifeOS AI understands a user's goals, current situation, available time, strengths, weaknesses, and schedule.

The AI generates a personalized roadmap, daily timetable, habits, milestones, and adapts continuously until the goal is achieved.

The app should feel like a personal AI coach rather than a tracker.
